Vona helps developers and entrepreneurs validate ideas quickly with a ready-to-use, dependency-free UI library for simple landing pages and email signups.

    • Launch Pages. Instantly.

    Vona offers a collection of ready-made, minimalist UI blocks built for fast, no-fuss landing pages. Drop them into your HTML and focus on your product—not your CSS.

    Prebuilt Blocks

    From hero sections to feature lists, Vona gives you well-designed building blocks you can mix, match, and customize in seconds. No design skills required.

    Drop-in Simplicity

    No bundlers, no setup, no friction. Just copy the markup and you're live. Perfect for makers, hackers, and early-stage projects.

    Lightweight by Design

    Vona stays out of your way. It's minimal, class-light, and built to complement Pico—not replicate it. Your HTML stays readable and your pages stay fast.
